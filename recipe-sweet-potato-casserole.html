Sweet Potato Casserole

From the kitchen of Terri Board

← Back to All Recipes

Sweet potato casserole with a pecan streusel topping sits at the intersection of Southern and Midwestern holiday cooking — a dish that crossed the Mason-Dixon line during the 20th century and became a fixture at Thanksgiving tables across the country. Terri Board's version is rich and satisfying, with the crunch of pecans against the creamy sweet potato base.

Ingredients

  • 3 c mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1 c brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ c melted butter
  • ½ c milk
  • Topping: ½ c brown sugar, 1/3 c flour, 1/3 c melted butter, 1 c chopped pecans

Instructions

  1. Combine first 6 ingredients. Pour into a buttered 1½–2 quart casserole dish.
  2. Mix topping ingredients together and sprinkle over top.
  3. Bake at 350° for 30–40 minutes until hot and browned.
