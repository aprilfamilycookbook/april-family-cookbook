Salad Dressing

From the kitchen of Mary April
← Back to All Recipes

A tangy, sweet homemade salad dressing made with tomato soup, sugar, oil, vinegar, and garlic. Shakes together in a jar in seconds — no blender needed. The tomato soup gives it a creamy, slightly sweet base while the vinegar and garlic keep it lively.

Ingredients

  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 c sugar
  • 1 c salad oil
  • ½ c vinegar
  • ½ tsp garlic, or more to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a container with a tight-fitting lid.
  2. Shake well until fully combined.
  3. Refrigerate any leftovers.
  4. Shake again before each use.
Adjust to taste: The garlic amount is flexible — start with ½ tsp and add more to your preference. Keeps well in the refrigerator for several weeks.
