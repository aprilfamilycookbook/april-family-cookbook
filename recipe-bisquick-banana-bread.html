Bisquick One-Pan Banana Bread

From the kitchen of Mom (Mary April)

← Back to All Recipes

Bisquick, introduced in 1931, represented a genuine revolution in American home baking — a pre-mixed flour blend that put quick breads within reach of c...

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 c vegetable oil
  • ½ c mashed ripe bananas
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • 2⅓ c Bisquick
  • ½ c chopped nuts

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350°. Generously grease bottom of loaf pan (9×5×3).
  2. Stir all ingredients in pan with fork until moistened. Beat vigorously 1 minute.
  3. Bake until toothpick comes out clean, 55 to 65 minutes.
  4. Run knife around sides of loaf to loosen. Remove from pan.
